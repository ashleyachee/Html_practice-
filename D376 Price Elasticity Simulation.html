Price Change Explorer

Explore how a price change can affect customer demand, revenue, and contribution margin. The goal is not to find one perfect price, but to see the tradeoffs marketers evaluate when pricing decisions change.

Current Situation

A company is reviewing the monthly price for a premium coffee subscription.

Current price $20/month
Current customers 1,000
Current revenue $20,000

Choose a Pricing Decision

Select one option to see a possible customer response and marketing interpretation.

Results

Increase price by 5%

  • New price: $21/month
  • Estimated customers: 970
  • Estimated revenue: $20,370
  • Estimated contribution margin: $9,670
Marketing insight

Customer demand decreases slightly, but the higher price may offset the smaller customer base. This can work when customers still perceive strong value in the offer.
