Scenario

Business Scenario

A regional grocery chain has seen a decline in customer loyalty over the past year. Competition from online retailers has increased, operating costs are rising, and customers are expressing concerns about product availability, convenience, and sustainability practices.

STEEPLED context to consider

A brief STEEPLED scan can help identify external factors that may inform the SWOT analysis:

  • Social and demographic: Customers want convenient shopping options, reliable product availability, and visible sustainability efforts.
  • Technological: Online retailers and delivery platforms are raising expectations for digital ordering, inventory visibility, and fast fulfillment.
  • Economic: Rising operating costs and price-sensitive customers may pressure margins and loyalty.
  • Environmental, legal, and ethical: Sustainability claims, sourcing practices, packaging, and food safety expectations may affect customer trust.
