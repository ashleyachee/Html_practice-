How the elements work together

The cultural web works as a system, not as six separate parts. Stories, symbols, routines, power, structure, and controls all support and reinforce one another. For example, people with influence may shape routines, routines may strengthen certain stories, and controls may reward the same behaviors that the structure and symbols already signal as important. Looking at all of the elements together helps students see how culture is created, maintained, and sometimes changed.
