STEEPLED Analysis

STEEPLED analysis helps leaders scan the external environment and identify forces that may create opportunities, risks, or pressure for strategic change.

How to Use STEEPLED

Use the framework to examine eight external forces that influence organizational strategy.

  1. Scan: Identify major trends outside the organization.
  2. Interpret: Explain how those trends may affect the organization.
  3. Apply: Use findings to support strategic decisions.

STEEPLED Categories

  • S: Social
  • T: Technological
  • E: Economic
  • E: Environmental
  • P: Political
  • L: Legal
  • E: Ethical
  • D: Demographic

Social

Core idea: Social forces shape customer expectations, brand perceptions, workplace culture, and consumer preferences.

Ask: How are changing values or behaviors affecting what customers or employees expect?

Core idea: Technology can change how organizations create value, communicate, compete, and serve customers.

Core idea: Economic conditions influence demand, pricing, hiring, borrowing, investment, and organizational stability.

Ask: How might economic trends affect demand, costs, or strategic priorities?

Core idea: Environmental factors include resource use, waste, sustainability expectations, and physical environmental risks.

Ask: What environmental pressures could affect operations, supply chains, or reputation?

Political

Core idea: Political decisions can influence taxation, trade, public funding, regulation, and market conditions.

Ask: Are political decisions creating new risks, restrictions, or opportunities?

Legal

Show detailsHide details

Core idea: Legal factors shape what organizations must, may, or may not do.

Ask: What legal requirements could increase costs, require process changes, or create risk?

E

Ethical

Fairness, responsibility, transparency, and stakeholder trust.

Show detailsHide details

Core idea: Ethical factors go beyond legality and focus on what stakeholders view as responsible and trustworthy.

Ask: Would stakeholders see this decision as fair, responsible, and aligned with organizational values?

D

Demographic

Population characteristics and workforce or market composition.

Show detailsHide details

Core idea: Demographic factors include age, income, education, location, migration, and population change.

Main Takeaway

STEEPLED helps leaders connect external trends to strategic impact. Strong analysis does more than name a trend; it explains why the trend matters and how the organization might respond.
